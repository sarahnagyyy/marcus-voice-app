Marcus Aurelius Companion

🗣️ How to Use This App

  • Click 🎙️ Start Speaking to begin recording.
  • Ask your question aloud within the 5-second window.
  • Wait a few seconds, then Marcus Aurelius will respond with Stoic wisdom—spoken and written.

Note: If audio does not play, ensure your microphone is enabled & browser allows autoplay or try refreshing.
